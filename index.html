About Me

After majoring in Finance and Real Estate and Florida State University, Matt Gallagher landed a job with CBRE, Inc. in Chicago. Working as a Commercial Real Estate Appraiser for three years, Matt gained invaluable experience providing valuation and advisory on institutional-grade office towers throughout the Midwest.


Most recently, Matt accepted a job with Hines Interests; where he works as an Analyst on their Multifamily Development team. His responsibilities include underwriting of potential developments, market analysis, and asset management of current developments under ownership.


In his free time, Matt enjoys traveling, coding, and anything involving sports - specifically Florida State football.
